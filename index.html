About ACT North Campus

Shaping Tomorrow's
Technology Leaders

Asian College of Technology – North Campus is a premier institution dedicated to providing high-quality, industry-aligned education in technology, engineering, business, and the sciences. Since our founding, we have stood at the forefront of academic innovation in Asia.

"Our mission is to cultivate competent, ethical, and globally competitive graduates who contribute meaningfully to society and the advancement of technology."

Located in the heart of the northern district, our campus features state-of-the-art laboratories, collaborative study spaces, and direct industry partnerships that bridge the gap between academia and the professional world.

🎓

CHED Accredited

All programs are accredited by the Commission on Higher Education, meeting the highest standards of quality assurance.

🔬

Research-Driven

Active research centers and innovation hubs that produce groundbreaking work in applied technology.

🌏

Global Network

Partner universities and companies across Asia, Europe, and the Americas for exchange programs and internships.
